Friendly tips:

1.      Make WIFI connection to home / office network one by one:

If you have more than 1 LED lamp, please power on one LED lamp and make successful connection first, then power on another LED lamp and make connection. Otherwise you may make connection of one LED lamp to another LED lamp.

2.      How to identify each single LED lamp:

In case you have several LED lamps, you can click single LED lamp IP for 3 seconds on online device list, related LED lamp will flash 3 times:

This function also works for “Add new group” UI in order to identify single LED lamp.

3.      How to identify LED lamps for each group:

In case you have several LED groups, you can click group title for 3 seconds as below and all the LED lamps which in this group will flash 3 times.

 

Explanation of operation:

Part 1: Software Downloading and Installation:

1.      Download from APPSTORE or GOOGLEPLAY:

Searching APP named “LED-GROW” on APPSTORE or GOOGLEPLAY to download the software;

2.      Scan QR code on packing box to download software.

3.      Run APP after successful installation:

Part 2: Connection to LED Lamp

This APP supports two ways for WIFI connection between mobile and LED lamp.

Way 1. Connection via home / office network:

1). Power on LED lamp;

2). Keep your mobile phone connects to home/ office network;

3). Click icon “   ”:

4). Click “Add new device”, APP will search and show device available as following, and click target device network to make connection between mobile and target device:

5). Choose and click home/ office network:

6). Input access password of home/ office network and then click “Connect”:

7). Please wait around 1 minute after APP reconfigures WIFI modem on LED lamp (WIFI is rebooting), and then click “Scan” on main UI to search device connected to home/ office network:

 

Way 2. Direct WIFI connection between mobile phone and LED lamp:

If home/ office network is not available, you can make direct WIFI connection between mobile phone and LED lamp:

1). Find WIFI of LED lamp through WIFI selection of Mobile phone;

2). Choose WIFI named “CTLite-xxxx” and input password “12345678” and click “connect”;

3). Run APP and make control of LED lamp.

3.     Delete device:

1). Delete by APP:

A. Click “Delete device”; B. Select target device(s); C. Click “Ok”.

2). Delete by hardware reset:

Press red button beside antenna for 15 seconds, WIFI modem will reset automatically.

After successful WIFI reset, G4 light is deleted from home / office network.

Part 3: Grouping

If you have more than one LED lamps connected, you can add new group and assign LED lamp(s) to new group, and you can control different group individually:

1.      Add new group:

1). Click “Add new group”:

2). Click targeted LED lamp on left side and targeted LED lamp to jump to new group:

3). Click “SAVE”

2. Delete Group:

1). Click “Delete group”;  2). Select targeted group;  3). Click “OK”

Part 4: Control of LED lamps

1.      Click “Control ” icon on right side of related group to enter control UI:

Please note:

1)     “Clone” on top and left corner means LED light is working under clone mode;

2)     Curve chart is data sent from LED light to mobile, which means LED light is working under this curve chart in a day.

2.      Working mode available and how to activate certain working mode:

1). “Seedling”, “Clone”, “Vegetation”, “Flowering”, “Fruiting”, “Self-def.” and “Instant” working modes are available;

2). Click certain working mode to activate this working mode on LED lights. 

3.      How to make setting for certain working mode:

1). How to choose time point:

Slide on chart left and right with finger to choose time point;

2). Add time point:

- Click “+”:

-        Adjust each LED channel to percentage you want;

-        Click “Ok”

2). Delete time point:

-  Click “〈”  /  “〉” to choose time point;

-  Click “-” to delete chosen time point;

3). Edit time point:

-  Slide vertical clock line to existing time point or click “〈” /  “〉” to choose time point;

-  Click “Edit”

-  Adjust percentage of each channel;

-  Click “Ok”

4). Restore to default setting:

Click “Default” to restore to default setting.

5). Preview:

Click “    ” to preview current setting.

4.      Instant mode:

Click “Instant” to enter instant mode and make related setting.

Part 5: Effect:

Please note: Effect is optional function only for user.

1.      Lightning:

1). Click to switch on / switch off lightning function;

2). Make setting of start time and end time for lightning function for each day;

2.      Acclimation:

1). Click to switch on / switch off acclimation function;

2). After switch on this function, LED will be working at 50% of current setting for the day you activate acclimation function, and will return to current setting the next day.
